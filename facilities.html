Our Facilities

THE RECEPTION AREA:
------Welcome to Net Core’s reception area. Here, you’ll find information about our custom-built PCs and services. Our team is ready to assist with any inquiries or orders. We focus on delivering high-quality, performance-driven computers tailored to your needs.

THE WORK AREA:
------The working area is dedicated to building the PCs you've ordered. Here, our skilled technicians assemble each system with attention to detail, ensuring your custom PC meets the highest standards of performance and quality.

THE BATHROOM AREA:
------We assure that the workers get their "Special Needs" and give them the breaks they needed and privacy in the of nature's calling.

THE STORAGEROOM:
------A secure, dry, and clean location to store the Computers and Parts for shipping.

THE OFFICE ROOM:
------The office room at Net Core is far from your traditional workspace—it's the heart of creativity and collaboration. This is where our team gathers to discuss new ideas, tackle challenges, and strategize the future of our custom-built PCs. It’s a space designed for open communication and problem-solving, where every discussion contributes to enhancing the products and services we offer. Whether we're brainstorming new technologies, fine-tuning design concepts, or planning the next big project, the office room is where innovation takes shape and decisions are made to bring your perfect PC to life.
